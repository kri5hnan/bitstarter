SPASMS is designed to give you the peace of mind while you are out on vacation and/or indoors to monitor the movements around your house. This product comes with a high resolution motion sensing camera concealed within a lamp arrangement that houses the LED lights which will turn on upon detecting any motion. SOLAR panels located on top of the lamp will charge the high efficient Lithium batteries, which are used to power upthe LED lamp and the camera. A free downloadable app that is currently available for the iPhone (which will be later expanded to Andriods) will be the console for accessing the video streams and photos capturing any movements outside your homes.

What is inside the box ?

  • SOLAR powered LED Lamp
  • High resolution motion sensing camera
  • High efficient Lithium batteries
  • Assembly Instructions
  • How to configure SPASMS with your WIFI network

FAQ

Is the product weather proof ?
This product is designed to withstand extreme temperatures and is made of high durable alloy that can take any amount of beating.
What is the life time of Lithium batteries ?
The expected life time of the Lithium batteries is 2 years. It is recommended to replace them upon receiving alert through your smartphone app.
What happens to the streaming, when there is a interruption with the WIFI network ?
SPASMS comes with a built-in memory that can hold upto roughly 8GB of videos/photos.
